History


"Lokmanya Nagar"! What an apt name for our society! The word Lokmanya itself suggests 'admired and accepted by the people' and the word 'Nagar' is suggestive of the idea of a residential society or premises. That is "Lokmanya Nagar". It is indeed our privilege that the name of our society is associated with the great erstwhile national leader and freedom fighter "Lokmanya Tilak". At the time of formation of our society, it was the intention of the founding members to provide for two-room tenements to middle-class persons from the small income groups.

The society was registered on 9th December 1946 under the then existing Co-operative Societies Act. It was only in 1948 that the construction of the buildings was started and the first batch of residents came to reside in the society in the year 1949, when four buildings were completed. It is interesting to note that the naming of the society took place on 19th December, 1953 under the auspices of the then Minister Bhausaheb Hire. The last of the buildings being "J" building was constructed in 1981. Since its inception, Lokmanya Nagar has been a happy witness to many social, religious and cultural activities thereby enriching the lives of its residents as well as of those living in the neighbourhood.

It is rightly said that "The society does not simply consist of the buildings with the walls and the roofs thereon. It really consists of its residents and the contributions made by them in the various spheres of human activities". Our society situated in the commercial capital i.e.Mumbai is like an oasis in a desert. As one enters the society, one is surrounded by green clustering trees and the buildings well planned, with sufficient space in between as well as adequate parking lots. The entire atmosphere is full of joy ,love and tranquillity. There is such a protective, warm and cozy feeling at the end of the day when one comes back tired and exhausted from office and enters the society. It is especially in the late evenings and during festivals and functions that one sees the real hustle bustle of the society. There cannot be a more social and busy platform than our "baks", as we affectionately call them! The Lokmanya Nagar society celebrated its 50th anniversary in December, 1996. We are now in the 65th year. Launching of a Web Site of our own society is a direction for forthcoming needs. However, one needs to remember that this is not the official web site of the society as such and is only a product of the efforts of some enthusiastic minds and talented people. Of course, it is only the support and encouragement from fellow members and residents that this web site shall continue to grow leaps and bounds.

Today, our society really resembles a big banyan tree with its numerous branches spread out in all directions and as far as the human eye can reach. So many of our members, their children, parents and relatives are settled in different parts of India and all over the world. Just like the world has shrunk in size due to the advancement in technology and satellite communication, it is time that our society comes closer not only in form but also in spirit. Wherever we all might be, ultimately we all shall continue to trace our roots back to Lokmanya Nagar. It is time that we create a common platform, where all of us proud Lokmanyaites can interact, communicate and be in touch in a much regular manner. Today, most of us are internet savy, have access to the latest technology and all we have to do is to make optimum use of our resources all over the world to evolve and nurture the ever lasting bond between us. The web site shall not only be a common platform for interaction and information sharing but it shall be our vehicle for displaying our unity amidst geographical diversity.

Wherever we are, UK or USA, Australia or Russia, UAE or Canada, let us all resolve to create, nurture and develop this web site and its nobles ideals and objectives.
