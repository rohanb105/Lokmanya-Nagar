Lokmanya Nagar Sabhagriha


The inauguration of the Lokmanya Nagar Sabhagriha was done on 20th November, 1982 at the hands of the then Chairman of the Maharashtra Legislative Council, Mr. Jayantrao Tilak. The hall was aptly named "Lokmanya Nagar Sabhagriha" at the said time. Also, all those who were involved in the construction and completion of the hall were felicitated

For the above occasion, there was a big feast i.e. "Sahabhojan" organised in the hall for all the residents of the society. It was a dream come true for all those who dreamt of having our own spacious community and marriage hall in the society. Today, the hall is used for various events both by residents of the society as well as outsiders. The hall can be rented for marriages and cultural events and booking for the same is done through the society office. This facility can be availed of by non-residents as well. Needless to say, in case the residents of the society wish to utilise the said hall, they are offered a concessional rate. From thread ceremonies to anniversaries, from birthdays to marriages, the hall is well utilised and put to optimum use. In fact, one witnesses so many marriages of residents of our society taking place in the said hall and once can proudly say that the hall has been a great boon to the society not only in terms of revenue generation but also in terms of availability for apt occasions.

Residents such as Mr. Sakharam Vitthal (Nana) Gokhale, Mr. Hemant Palande (of Ruchira Caterers) and Mrs. Yamini Vaidya have shown great spirit and entrepreneurial qualities by offering services in terms of food, catering, decoration etc for the various functions that take place in the hall.
