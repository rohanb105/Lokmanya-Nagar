Independence Day


The Independence Day in our country is celebrated on the 15th August every year. Even in our society, we organise a function to celebrate and commemorate this glorious event in the history of our nation. There is a flag hoisting ceremony which normally takes place at 8:30 a.m., in the centre of our society, at the hands of the President of our society. The national anthem is then sung by everybody present at that time. Later, sweets are distributed to all those present and coffee is served in the basement of the Lokmanya Nagar Sabhagriha.
