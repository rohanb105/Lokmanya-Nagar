Kojagiri Poornima


Just after Dassera, and before Diwali, the Kojagiri Poornima is celebrated in our society in grand style. It is one of the very few events in our society which witnesses a large gathering of the residents. Of course, the main attraction is the serving of "Masala milk", specially prepared for this occasion. This event is especially significant for all music lovers of our society since every year, to commemorate this night, a music programme is organised on a Saturday following the actual Kojagiri Poornima night. Usually, a professional troupe of musicians is invited to perform a live event especially that of Marathi sangeet,. In the interval, the Masala milk is served in the basement of the Lokmanya Nagar Sabhagriha. On this occasion, besides the monetary charges of the troupe, the performers are also felicitated by gifting them small bouquets at the hands of the Chairman of the Lokamanya Mandal.
