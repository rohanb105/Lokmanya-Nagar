Kailasvasi Tatyasaheb Oak Sabhagriha


"Kailasvasi Tatyasaheb Oak Sabhagriha", the small hall of the society is situated in the I building on the ground floor. Also used sometimes as the community hall, it was originally used as a play school premises for the then existing "Shishu Vihar". Earlier in the evenings (and now at any time in the day), it is utilised for social and cultural purposes such as family get togethers, babies naming ceremonies i.e. "barsa", community dinners etc. Just like the big hall, this hall is also available for both society residents and outsiders.
